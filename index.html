WHAT ARE THE
UK CORPORATE GAMES ?

The UK Corporate Games is Europe's largest multi-sport festival for businesses, that are open to all for the benefit of all. Companies of any size can enter and there are no restrictions on participants sporting ability. Every year thousands of participants represent hundreds of organisations in a weekend of unrivalled sports and celebrations.

We are delighted to be hosting the UK Corporate Games at Royal Holloway this year. We look forward to welcoming the competitors to our campus to make use of our fantastic sports facilities.
